TransLingo: Connecting English and Marathi

Transforming Conversations: Effective English-Marathi Translation and Summarization

Our NLP chatbot is designed to facilitate seamless communication between English and Marathi speakers. Leveraging advanced natural language processing techniques, the chatbot provides efficient translation and summarization services, ensuring users can communicate effectively, regardless of their language proficiency.

With the ability to translate text from English to Marathi and vice versa, the chatbot caters to the diverse needs of users. Additionally, it offers summarization features that allow users to condense lengthy paragraphs into concise summaries in both languages. This functionality is particularly beneficial for students, professionals, and anyone looking to enhance their understanding of content in a different language.

Key Features:
  • Real-time translation between English and Marathi.
  • Summarization of English and Marathi texts for quick understanding.
  • User-friendly interface for easy interaction and access.
  • Available 24/7 to assist with translation and summarization needs.
